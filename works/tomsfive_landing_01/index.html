Services & Our Product

Get to know each other. Efficient, authentic and simple.
helping people to find people





Tomsfive is a recruiting tool that enables you to focus on the person. Two-sided and interactive. We strive to a disruption in the recruiting process, in favor of the personal fit and your company's culture! Start building your winning teams! Start to hire for attitude and train for skills!

Personal fit

Focus on things that really matter. Consider candidates that fit in your team and company culture, not only focus on CVs and diploma.

User-controlled

Recruiting is, and most likely will always be, a personal task and of a machine. Let your recruiter and the candidates decide about a match.

Highly efficient

Meet 20 to 30 appropriate candidates daily - face to face. Your applicant database and your network of candidates will dramatically increase in quality. .
